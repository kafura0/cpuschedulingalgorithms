1. Open Command Prompt.
2. Enter "java FCFS" into the command line.
3. You will be prompted with "Enter number of processes: ". Proceed to enter the number of processes.
4. You will be prompted with "Enter Burst Time for each process". Proceed to enter the burst time for each process one by one.
5. You will be prompted with "Enter Around Time". Proceed to enter the around time for each process one by one.
6. Finally, you will be shown the results of the program.
